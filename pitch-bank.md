# TSP Pitch Brief — Three Brothers Bank

## The question that opens the conversation

> "Is your business structured for AI to work inside it?"

For a bank, this question has a second layer: *"And can you prove to an examiner that it's structured correctly?"*

Most institutions can't answer either question. AI tools designed for general business don't understand compliance boundaries. They treat a loan officer's email the same as a marketing newsletter. They have no concept of what data can leave your network and what can't.

TSP builds the structure — and builds it for regulated environments.

## What this looks like for your institution

You have 300 employees. Their daily work involves customer data, loan files, compliance documents, and internal communications. Each of those categories carries different handling requirements. A staff AI tool that doesn't understand those distinctions isn't a productivity tool — it's a liability.

**RHONDA** is your staff-facing AI — deployed entirely within your network. No customer data leaves your infrastructure. No PII touches a third-party model. Your staff gets AI-assisted drafting, document summarization, and operational support. Your examiners see a clean audit trail.

**LEO** is your executive intelligence layer — for the three of you. Your business decisions, your institutional knowledge, your strategic context. Advisory-grade intelligence that reflects how you've run this bank and where you're taking it. This is cloud-based — no customer data involved, only your own thinking and strategy.
